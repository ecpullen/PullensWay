Use this page for contact details, a note about how often updates happen, and a short invitation for readers who want to learn more.

## What to include

- Best contact method
- Expected response time
- Whether people can request route suggestions or local advice
- Any newsletter, social profile, or email list you plan to add later

## Tone suggestion

Keep it direct and welcoming. A guide site feels more trustworthy when the contact page is simple and human.
