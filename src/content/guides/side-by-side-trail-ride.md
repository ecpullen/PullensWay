This sample page should sell the ride itself: the terrain, the scenery, the pace, and the level of adventure. It works best when it clearly tells guests whether the trip is scenic, muddy, technical, or beginner-friendly.

## What guests want to know

- Ride duration
- Trail difficulty
- Clothing and eye-protection recommendations
- Age or passenger guidance

## Good sales copy to add later

Include the actual trail system, vehicle setup, stop points, and what kind of route makes this outing special.
